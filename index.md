## About Me

I'm a software engineer based in Madrid with a deep interest in building tools that feel invisible — software that stays out of the way and lets people focus on what matters.

Over the past seven years, I've worked across the stack, from low-level systems in Rust to browser-based interfaces in React. I care about performance, accessibility, and the small details that separate functional software from good software.

When I'm not writing code, I'm usually reading about typography, experimenting with generative art, or walking through the city with a podcast on.
